## 2. The app is a study aid — not a religious ruling

- **The zakat calculator.** An estimate, based on values and a nisab figure you enter
  yourself. Confirm your own obligation with a qualified scholar.
- **Prayer times.** Computed astronomically from your coordinates and your chosen
  calculation method, or fetched from a third-party service. Methods legitimately differ,
  and so do their results. Where your local mosque or authority differs, follow them.
- **Qibla direction.** A great-circle bearing, displayed through your device's
  magnetometer. Phone compasses are affected by magnetic interference, nearby metal and
  poor calibration. Confirm the direction by another means before relying on it.
- **Hijri dates, Ramadan and event dates.** Calculated from the Umm al-Qura calendar.
  Local moon-sighting may differ; confirm observances with your local authority.
- **Ruqyah, learning material, daily facts and thematic verse selections.** Study material,
